About Me

I'm a Full Stack Web Developer living in Atlanta, GA. I'm originally from New Orleans and attended the University of Georgia for my undergraduate degree where I majored in Psychology. Upon graduation I moved to Washington, D.C. and began working on Capitol Hill for David Vitter, a US Senator from Louisiana. After many years in the political realm I decided I was ready for a new challenge and found web development.

I spent a year teaching myself coding through online tutorials (and a lot of trial and error!) and building websites and games for myself and friends. I realized I wanted to pursue web development as a career and enrolled in DigitalCrafts' full-time immersive coding bootcamp in February of 2017.
